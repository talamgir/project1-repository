Five Reasons That We Need To Save The Whales

  • Since the inception of the 1986 Moratorium on Commercial Whaling, over 35,000 whales have been slaughtered for profit!
  • Right now, despite the worldwide moratorium on commercial whaling, fisherman from Japan, Norway, Iceland, and the Faroe Islands are going to kill over 25,000 dolphins, whales, and porpoises this year selling their meat for profit.
  • Since 1986, Japan has defied the moratorium by killing thousands of whales under the guise of “lethal scientific research”.
  • Norway has also defied the commercial whaling moratorium, killing over 500 minke whales per year as well.
  • Iceland is the latest nation to join the fray and has already killed over 100 endangered fin whales this summer!
